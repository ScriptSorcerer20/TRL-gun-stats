About Us

Hey there! You seem to have stumbled upon the TRL Stat Page created by Fretux and Losernoob124!


Our Mission

This website exists because we want to provide the TRL Community with an easy go-to way to calculate DPS and DPM values to help players make informed decisions about their gameplay. Whether you're fine-tuning your new Deathwish Strategy or simply curious about firearm performance, we’ve got you covered. If you have suggestions, feedback, or want to contribute new stats or features, we’d love to hear from you, just contact us on Discord or on the Suggestion Page!

Our Story

In the beginning, the only way to calculate DPS/DPM stats for certain operatives was, by using the Spreadsheet by Lanz1021 and a calculator, which quickly turned out to be (shockers) really annoying. TRL Stats started out as a side project, initially started by Losernoob124 to calculate gun stats more easily, but given up one after a couple of weeks. Eventually Fretux started working on it again and what initially was just a fun hobby grew into something much bigger, and through the help of Stack Overflow and many hours of programming, we turned it into a full-fledged website.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you calculate DPS and DPM?

We put the data given by Lanz1021's Stat Sheet into a .JSON (Javascript Object Notation) file, which we use to calculate the DPS (damage per second) and DPM (damage per minute) of each weapon via a formula that is also mentioned on Lanz1021' Spreadsheet.

How often are stats updated?

We update the weapon stats every time a new update is released for TRL.
